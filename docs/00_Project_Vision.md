# ResearchFlow AI

**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** July 2026

---

# Project Vision

## Overview

ResearchFlow AI is a production-ready multi-agent AI research platform designed to help users perform structured and reliable research.

Instead of relying on a single LLM response, the platform orchestrates multiple specialized AI agents that collaborate to understand a research query, gather information, verify findings, and generate a well-structured report with proper citations.

The goal is to provide a research workflow rather than just a conversational chatbot.

---

# Mission

Build an AI platform that transforms a simple research question into a structured, citation-backed research report through autonomous agent collaboration.

---

# Vision

To create an extensible AI platform that demonstrates modern AI Engineering principles including:

- Multi-Agent Systems
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Modular AI Architecture
- Production Backend Engineering
- Clean System Design

The project should be easily extensible to support additional AI applications beyond research.

---

# Problem Statement

Current AI chat applications require users to manually guide the research process by repeatedly asking follow-up questions, requesting summaries, and verifying information.

This process is:

- Time-consuming
- Difficult to organize
- Not transparent
- Poorly suited for long-form research
- Limited in document management

Users also struggle to combine information from personal documents and external sources into a single structured workflow.

---

# Proposed Solution

ResearchFlow AI automates the research workflow through multiple specialized AI agents.

Each agent is responsible for a single task such as planning, searching, retrieving, summarizing, or report generation.

This modular architecture improves maintainability, explainability, and future extensibility.

# Out of Scope (MVP)

The following features are intentionally excluded from the initial release:

- Voice assistant
- OCR for scanned documents
- Team collaboration
- Browser automation
- Mobile application
- MCP integrations
- Fine-tuning custom LLMs

These may be considered for future versions.

---

# Success Criteria

The MVP will be considered successful if users can:

- Create an account
- Start a research session
- Upload research documents
- Ask research questions
- Receive citation-backed answers
- Export structured reports
- View previous research sessions

---

# Long-Term Vision

ResearchFlow AI is designed as an AI platform rather than a single-purpose application.

Future applications built on the same platform may include:

- Resume Analysis
- Legal Research
- Medical Literature Review
- Financial Research
- Technical Documentation Assistant

The architecture should support these use cases with minimal changes.
